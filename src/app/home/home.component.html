Hi I'm Mucky Bottle! The recycling tracking system

I was dumped in a landfill much like this one in 1947 and have been gathering muck ever since. Each year I gain 22 billion siblings worldwide. I have spent my tiresome existence trying to convince mortals to recycle when they can. I hope you learn something today!

About Us

MuckyBottle is a multi-platform software inteded to work on a web-browser or Google Home. With convenience as the forefront design philosophy, information about the recycling process is a spoken word away!

 How to get involved
  1. Begin by checking out the sample phrases you can ask the Mucky Bottle.
  2. Check your local recycling times on the Stats page.
  3. Add your recycled material into your personal user database on the My Recycling page.
  4. Take pride in knowing you are preventing the accumulation of Mucky Bottles everywhere!
