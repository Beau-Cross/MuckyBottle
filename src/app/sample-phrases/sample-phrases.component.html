How to use the Google Assistant App

Begin your inquiry with the phrase:
"Talk to Mucky Bottle"

 Next, ask it something like...
  • What should I recycle?
  • Can you recycle a battery?
  • Should I recycle a bottle?
  • Where can I recycle?
  • What's your backstory?
 Mucky Bottle also speaks Spanish!
  • Que deberia reciclar?
  • Puedo reciclar una bateria?
  • Puedo reciclar una botella?
  • Donde puedo ir a reciclar?
  • Cuentame un poco de tu vida!
